What is the push pull leg system?

It is a training program that divides the body into 3 groups: the muscles of the upper body that can push, the muscles of the upper body that can pull, and the muscles of the lower part i.e. the legs.
This program is perhaps the most effective program because there are related muscle groups that are trained together in the same exercise.
This means that you get maximum overlap of movements in the same exercise, and the muscle groups that are trained get the overall benefit of this overlap.

Is a lean diet healthy?

A lean diet is not healthy, as the body needs fat for hair and skin, to absorb vitamins, and to provide vital essential fatty acids.

Therefore, it is recommended to introduce healthy fats to the diet, and replace foods rich in saturated fats with foods containing monounsaturated fats and polyunsaturated fats, such as: omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids.

Do I train in many groups and less weight or less groups and a big weight?

  • 1. Many people (especially women) wrongly believe that lifting heavy weights automatically leads to huge muscles, so they prefer lighter weights or no weights at all.
  • 2. The key factor is training to muscle fatigue, not the actual weight size — muscle growth depends on how you exercise, not just how heavy the weight is.
  • 3. Choosing the right weight isn’t about guessing or extremes; it should follow specific rules to avoid using too little (wasting effort) or too much (causing bad form).
